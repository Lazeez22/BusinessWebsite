Our Pee See Story



Where We Started

Pee see is a computer retail business founded on the values of convenience, accessibility, and excellent customer service. Believe it or not, this leader in local technology retail had pretty humble beginnings- when six students who were passionate about tech hunted down secondhand PCs in the Tipic PC online group to resell at decent rates.
Pee see officially got its name when we worked on our first website for a school activity. Its name was a key step in buildung a brand that promises reliable services and quality products. Eventually, we shifted to selling brand new products at accessible prices.


Where We're Going

Today, as we serve you online, our dedication to growth also extends to each and every customer. We strive to provide solutions, reliable services, and affordable but quality technologies that will enhance the lives of our customers and community.
As we move to greater heights, giving you the best customer experience is still our number one goal
